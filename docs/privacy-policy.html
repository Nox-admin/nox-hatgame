Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 9, 2026

Overview

Shlyapa ("Hat Game") is a word-guessing party game developed by Nox Labs Pte. Ltd. This policy explains how the app handles your data.

Data We Collect

None. The app does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. There are no user accounts, no analytics, no tracking, and no advertising.

Network Access

The app works entirely offline. It does not connect to the internet and does not send or receive any data.

Local Data

Game settings (language, preferences) are stored locally on your device using standard iOS storage. This data never leaves your device and is deleted when you uninstall the app.

Third-Party Services

The app does not use any third-party services, SDKs, or analytics tools.

Children's Privacy

The app does not collect any data from anyone, including children.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, we will update it on this page with a new effective date.
